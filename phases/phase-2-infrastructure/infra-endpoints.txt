# Infrastructure Service Endpoints

## Kafka Service
- Internal Endpoint: kafka.kafka.svc.cluster.local:9092
- Service Name: kafka-service
- Namespace: kafka

## PostgreSQL Service
- Internal Endpoint: postgresql.postgres.svc.cluster.local:5432
- Service Name: postgresql-service
- Namespace: postgres

## Notes
- These endpoints are for internal cluster communication
- External access would require LoadBalancer or Ingress configuration
- Authentication and SSL settings may be required for secure connections
